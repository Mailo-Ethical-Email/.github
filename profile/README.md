## What is Mailo?

Mailo is an ethical, privacy-first email suite hosted in Europe (France) that provides email, cloud storage, calendar, address book, chat, and videoconferencing services in a single platform [citation:1][citation:3]. Founded and operated by Mail Object SAS, Mailo is designed to protect your personal data and privacy while offering a complete set of communication and productivity tools.

Unlike mainstream email providers, Mailo displays no advertising on paid plans, does not track your activity, and is committed to reducing its environmental footprint [citation:2][citation:3]. The service meets everyone's needs—individuals, families, children, professionals, associations, schools, and town halls [citation:2][citation:3]. With multiple account types ranging from free to premium and professional plans, Mailo offers flexibility for any use case [citation:6][citation:12].

## Installation Guide

### Option 1: Webmail Access (No Installation)

Mailo is accessible from any modern browser on Windows.

**Step 1:** Visit `https://www.mailo.com` in your browser.

**Step 2:** Click Sign Up and choose your plan (Free, Premium, or Pro).

**Step 3:** Enter your personal details and complete registration.

**Step 4:** Start using Mailo's webmail and productivity suite immediately.

### Option 2: Email Client Setup (IMAP/SMTP)

**IMAP Settings (Incoming Mail):**

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Server | `imap.mailo.com` |
| Port | `993` (SSL/TLS) |
| Username | Your full Mailo email address |
| Password | Your Mailo password |

**SMTP Settings (Outgoing Mail):**

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Server | `smtp.mailo.com` |
| Port | `587` (STARTTLS) |
| Authentication | Required |
| Username | Your full Mailo email address |
| Password | Your Mailo password |

### Option 3: Mobile App

Mailo offers mobile apps for iOS and Android with push notifications, address book sync, and full access to all Mailo features [citation:2][citation:3].

### Option 4: Mailo Junior (For Children)

Create a secure email address for your child with parental supervision [citation:5]:

**Step 1:** Visit `https://junior.mailo.com`.

**Step 2:** Create your child's email address under your supervision.

**Step 3:** Manage your child's address book and approved contacts.

**Step 4:** Choose age-appropriate interface (6-9: mini interface; 10-14: junior interface) [citation:5].
